Article 1, The different types of mental health problems.

Mental Health: Mental Health is a big issue these days. It affects how we think ,feel, and sometimes it even affects how we act. Your mood and behavior are all affected! Many factors contribute to Mental Health Problems, some could be, The loss of a loved one Addictions Bullying School related problems Stress Many things contribute to Mental Health, you should make sure that you identify the symptoms if you are, Eating or sleeping too much or too little Having low energy, pushing away activities Feeling helpless and alone often Experiencing severe mood swings Inability to perform daily tasks (like getting to school) Finding help on a website like this or talking to a trusted person can help you recover. If trying to recover you should start to feel like your overall health and wellness is improving. You should also strive to achieve your full potential. If going through Mental Health problems, you should create a recovery plan where you can identify goals for yourself to achieve, this will help you recover faster. If you are wondering whether children can have Mental Health problems, they definitely can! It is important all issues are addressed before the issue gets worse.
